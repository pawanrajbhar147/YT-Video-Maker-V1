### 001
BRICS meeting chal rahi thi, aur Beijing ne chupke se ek aisi chal chali jisse dollar nahi, balki poora India fanas jata. Lekin ek aisi palatwar hui jiski umeed kisi ko nahi thi.

### 002
Yeh koi aam meeting nahi thi, yeh ek bada jaal tha. Agar yeh chal kamyab ho jaati, toh hamare desh ki azaadi aur banking system seedhe padosi desh ke control me ja sakte the.

### 003
Aap soch rahe honge ki isse aapki life par kya asar padta? Jab aisi global chal chali jaati hai, toh aapke bank account me rakhe paise ki keemat aur mehangai turant badal jaati hai.

### 004
Aaj is video me hum poori kahani ko decode karenge aur samjhenge ki kaise India ne ek bade currency trap yani paison ke jaal ko hamesha ke liye nakam kar diya.

### 005
Kahani shuru hoti hai ek bade sapne se, jise duniya kehti hai de-dollarization. Is mushkil shabd ka aasan matlab hai—apne vyapar me dollar ka istemaal band ya kam karna.

### 006
Duniya bhar ke naye aur tezi se badhte desh ek naye group me shamil hue jise hum BRICS kehte hain. Is saal, yani do hazaar chhabis me, is group ki kaman India ke haath me hai.

### 007
Is naye group ka maqsad bohot bada tha. Duniya ke bade desh chahte the ki business karne ke liye unhe kisi ek desh ke dabdabe ke aage jhukna na pade.

### 008
Lekin is group ke andar ek aur bada khiladi baitha hai, jiska maqsad dollar ko hatana toh tha, par duniya par apna hukka chalana bhi tha. Uska target tha apni khud ki currency ko boss banana.

### 009
Yahan par pehla bada sawal khada hota hai jo aapko sochna chahiye. Agar dollar badalta hai, toh kya uski jagah kisi aise padosi ki currency aani chahiye jo hamesha dhokha deta hai?

### 010
Hum yahan do bade sawalon ka jawab dhoondenge. Pehla, ki Beijing ne yeh currency ka jaal kaise buna? Aur doosra, ki hamare desh ka is khel me ultimate ya aakhri endgame kya hai?

### 011
Is takraav ko samajhne ke liye hume un khufiya dastavezon aur meetings ke andar jhankna hoga, jahan har ek desh apni chal chalne ke liye poori tarah taiyar baitha tha.

### 012
Beijing ka plan bada hi seedha aur khatarnak tha. Unhone kaha ki jab hum aapas me business karte hain, toh hum baar-baar American dollar ka rasta kyun dekhein? Hume apna naya paisa banana chahiye.

### 013
Unhone ek aisi nayi currency ka idea samne rakha jo poori tarah se unke khud ke control me ho. Iska matlab tha ki jo bhi desh isme shamil hoga, use unke rules manne padenge.

### 014
Isko kehte hain currency hegemony, yani aasan bhasha me, paise ke dum par doosre deshon ko dabana. Jaise mohalle me ek hi baniya sabko apni sharton par udhaar deta hai.

### 015
Beijing chahta tha ki BRICS ke naye sadasya, jaise Saudi Arabia ya Iran, apna saara tel aur gas American dollar ke bajay unki currency, yani Yuan me bechna shuru kar dein.

### 016
Agar aisa ho jata, toh poori duniya ka petrol aur gas ka market unke ishare par naachne lagta. Aur India ko har chhote-bade sauda karne ke liye unpar nirbhar hona padta.

### 017
Lekin yahan ek bada mod aaya. Kisi bhi naye currency ko chalane ke liye poore group ki sahamati chahiye hoti hai. Aur yahan par sabse uncha aur mazboot pashan bankar khada tha India.

### 018
Chaliye ek chhota sa break lete hain aur dekhte hain ki ab tak humne kya samjha. Beijing ne jo jaal buna tha, woh poori tarah se taiyar tha, par unhe umeed nahi thi ki aage kya hoga.

### 019
Hamare desh ne is meeting me saaf kar diya ki hum kisi bhi aise naye system ka hissa nahi banenge jo hamari strategic autonomy, yani hamari apni marzi ke faisle lene ki azaadi ko chhene.

### 020
New Delhi ka rasta bilkul alag tha. Unhone kaha ki agar dollar par nirbharta kam karni hai, toh kisi naye raja ko chunne ki zaroorat nahi hai. Hum apni khud ki local currencies me vyapar karenge.

### 021
Isko kehte hain local currency settlement system, matlab jab do desh aapas me business karein, toh woh ek-doosre ke asli rupaye aur paise ka istemaal karein, kisi teesre ki zaroorat na ho.

### 022
Jaise agar India aur UAE aapas me business kar rahe hain, toh wo Rupaye aur Dirham me len-den karenge. Isme na dollar beech me aayega aur na hi Beijing ka Yuan.

### 023
Yeh move itna bada jhatka tha kyunki isne Beijing ke us poore plan ki hawa nikal di, jisme woh khud ko dollar ka iklauta vikalp ya replacement dikha rahe the.

### 024
Is faisle ne baaki deshon ko bhi ek naya rasta dikha diya. Unhe samajh aaya ki bina kisi naye gulam bane bhi dollar ke dabdabe se bacha ja sakta hai.

### 025
Chaliye ab is poore pattern ke dots ko aapas me jodte hain aur dekhte hain ki duniya ke is bade chessboard par kaun sa khiladi kahan khada hai aur kya chahta hai.

### 026
Beijing ka endgame bada saaf hai—woh dollar ko hatakar har ek global trade route par apna kabza chahte hain taaki poori duniya unki sharton par business karne ko majboor ho jaye.

### 027
America ka endgame yeh hai ki woh kisi bhi keemat par dollar ka dabdaba banaye rakhna chahte hain aur BRICS ke har ek naye kadam par bohot hi gehri aur shaqqi nigah rakh rahe hain.

### 028
Aur hamare pyaare desh India ka endgame sabse shandar hai—hum ek aisi multi-aligned duniya chahte hain jahan koi ek boss na ho, aur har desh barabari se aage badh sake.

Ab faisla aapke haath me hai, aapko kya lagta hai, kya India ka yeh local currency wala rasta duniya ko ek behtar aur barabar ka mauka dega? Neeche comment me zaroor batayein.
